A little about us

At the moment, when employees travel as part of their job description or work on deployment in another country, they are usually covered to a certain extent by company expenses.
Perfect Incentive have come up with a solution for keeping track of employees expenses which allows the company to see exactly what company expenses are being spent on by their employees.
The app that we will create will allow the user to capture an image of their receipts which will extract the information from the receipt and send that data back to a database. The database will recognise some food & drink items and other items will be learned by the database for future use so that the app will always be progressive.
